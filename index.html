Mission

Michael the Debugger is a game where you have to help Michael to defeat the bugs. Collect coins and spray cans to defeat the enemies. The game is over when you have no more lives left. Good luck!

How to Play

Use the left and right buttons to move the character. Jump with the jump button and shoot with the fire button. Collect coins and spray cans to defeat the enemies. The game is over when you have no more lives left. Good luck!
